# Future Features

Ideas brainstormed during planning — not in scope for v1, but worth revisiting.

---

## Full Remote Control Dashboard

A web app (Mac/phone) that combines browsing your film & TV collection with full Apple TV remote control — navigation, app launching, playback — all from a single interface. The archive acts as the source of truth for what to watch.

## Browse + Quick Launch (Web)

Keep the archive's browsing and filtering experience in the browser, but add a "Watch on Apple TV" button per title. Clicking it searches the Apple TV for that title and opens it directly, without requiring full remote control.

## tvOS-Style Interface Redesign (Web)

Redesign archive.html to look and feel like a native tvOS app — large poster focus cards, top navigation bar, cinematic full-screen backgrounds — while keeping it as a browser page.
